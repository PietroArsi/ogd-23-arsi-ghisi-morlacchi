<b>Game Installation</b>

Unleash the purr-fection by downloading Catnipped! from Steam onto your Windows device. Ensure you have enough room for this "meow-nificent" adventure and check the system requirements for a smooth gameplay experience.

<b>Starting the Game</b>

Spot the Catnipped! icon on your device, give it a playful paw tap to launch the game. Get ready for some feline fun!
You'll be greeted with a splash screen or an introductory video, depending on whether it's your first playthrough or not. Sit tight and let the catxcitement build as the game loads.

<b>Main Menu</b>

After loading, you'll be taken to the main menu of Catnipped! Prepare to be whiskered away to various options and menus, including:

- Host: Ready to host a lobby and rule the kitty underworld?
- Join: Join forces with fellow fur-riends in a purr-suit of adventure?
- Extra: Unleash some extra surprises and secrets that will make you purr with delight!
- Tutorial: Hey, that's me! Check this menu for helpful tips and tricks to conquer Catnipped!
- Options: Tailor the game settings to your liking and make it truly meow-gical.

<b>Lobby Menu</b>

Once you click the "Join" button you will find yourself in the Lobby Selection Screen. Choose a purrfectly paw-some lobby to join and embark on your escapades.
 
<b>Level Selection Menu</b>

Once every member of your mischievous crew has selected their cunning catnip-loving character, it's time to embark on the Level Selection Screen. Gather in the CatCar and paw-sitively choose the level where your adventures will unfold. Please note that in this version, the host will be the sole master of the van's steering wheel.

<b>Gameplay</b>

Finally some gameplay! Here are the controls that will make you a whisker-wielding master:
- Movement: Use WASD keys to gracefully navigate the world.
- Camera Movement: Use the mouse to find the perfect angle.
- Interaction: Left-click like a pawfessional gamer to interact with objects and characters (Scratch).
- Cut: Press E to use your claws and prepare some delicious catnip ready for storage.
- Open Menu: Press Q to reveal the Construction Menu for delightful surprises.
- Meow: Express yourself with a mighty meow by pressing M.
- Menu Navigation: Use the mouse or WASD to navigate the menus with cat-like grace.

In Catnipped! your goal is to produce as much catnip as possible in the time given by each level, meow-organize with other feline fellows to maximize your catnip production.
Keep an eye out for mischievous mice scurrying about, as catching them will earn you extra points. 
At the end of each level, a generous serving of "crunchies" (1, 2, or 3) will be awarded based on your catnip production and mouse-catching prowess.
But beware! The P.U.P.S. (Paws United for Protecting Society), a barking-mad group of puppies, will stop at nothing to snatch your precious catnip and land you in the doghouse. Use your mighty scratching powers to fend them off and catch those delicious mice!	
Want to fortify your defenses? Access the construction menu and strategically place traps to keep your turf secure. But remember, in the underground world, everything has a price! Using these traps an alarm will play when a pup passes through it. You can also buy some pieces of wall to deter the puppies from accessing the catnip from some specific angles.
To produce catnip, harvest the tantalizing plants from the field, cook them in the FURnace, slice them on the cutting table, and store them safely in the storage box. 
The level comes to an end when time runs out or if the Pawlice gathers enough evidence (catnip) to put the cuffs on you.
Keep a keen eye on the evidence counter and stay one step ahead!

<b>Results Screen</b>

Once you've conquered a level, your score will be presented in the form of mouth-watering "crunchies" on the results screen. Purr-haps you'll earn the highest honor of a three-crunchie rating!

<b>Saving Your Progress</b>

The game implements automatic saving to avoid losing your progress. We don't wanna lose any life here, even if we have nine of them.

<b>Have a Pawsome Time!</b>

Now that you're fully equipped with the knowledge of Catnipped!, it's time to dive into the enchanting world of mischievous cats and addictive catnip. Explore, interact, complete daring levels, and enjoy the "claw-some" adventures that await you.

Get ready for a "fur-tastic" time with Catnipped! Let the feline frenzy begin!
